The best movies according to Joshua Barnachea

My top 3 movies of all time.

Avengers Infinity War

Avengers: Infinity War is a top movie because it delivers an epic and emotional experience that brings together an unprecedented number of beloved Marvel characters to face their greatest threat yet. The film's action sequences are visually stunning and expertly choreographed, with each hero bringing their unique abilities to the fight against the powerful and imposing villain Thanos. Beyond the impressive spectacle, the movie also offers strong character development and compelling relationships between the heroes, making the audience deeply invested in their struggles and sacrifices. The devastating ending, which leaves audiences stunned and eager for the next installment, cements Avengers: Infinity War as a cinematic masterpiece that showcases the best of the superhero genre.

Avengers Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is a top movie because it delivers an incredibly satisfying conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's first decade of storytelling. The film successfully manages to balance humor, heart, and epic action sequences while paying off years of character development and fan theories. The stakes are higher than ever as the surviving Avengers band together to undo the devastating consequences of the previous film, culminating in a climactic battle that is both visually stunning and emotionally powerful. The film's conclusion is bittersweet, as beloved characters say goodbye and move on to new phases of their lives. The death of Tony Stark, in particular, is a heartbreaking moment that brings the entire MCU full circle, leaving many fans, including yourself, in tears. Overall, Avengers: Endgame is a cinematic triumph that rewards loyal fans and casual moviegoers alike with a thrilling and emotional experience.

The Pursuit of Happyness

The Pursuit of Happyness is a top movie because it tells a poignant and inspiring story of perseverance and hope in the face of adversity. Based on a true story, the film follows Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman and single father who, despite facing seemingly insurmountable obstacles, never gives up on his dream of providing a better life for his son. Will Smith delivers a powerhouse performance that captures the emotional depth and complexity of Gardner's journey, while also showcasing his resilience and determination. The film's themes of family, sacrifice, and the American Dream resonate deeply with audiences, making it a timeless classic that continues to inspire and uplift viewers. Overall, The Pursuit of Happyness is a masterful work of storytelling that reminds us of the power of the human spirit to overcome even the most challenging circumstances.
